Description view

When you first enter a location you are given a brief description of where you are. This description will also contain the results of your last action.

To progress to the action view, click the "OK" link. How you do this will vary depending on the make of your mobile phone.

If you don't do anything for about 20 seconds, the screen will automatically refresh itself, informing you of anything that has happened in the meantime.
