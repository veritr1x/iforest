Creating a new character

To start playing, you need to create a character. Point your WAP browser at wap.littlescreen.co.uk, and choose the "iForest" option.

At the login prompt, choose a name for your character, and a password. Then click "New character". If your password was less than 5 letters long, or if your character name has already been taken, you will be asked to try again.

Choosing an unusual name for your character is less likely to conflict with anybody else's name. If you are having trouble, try adding numbers to the end.

After successfully choosing a name and password, you will be taken to the game. Bookmark your position on your browser straight away, as this will let you come back without having to login again.
