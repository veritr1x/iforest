Rules

When you are playing iForest, there are some simple rules and conditions that you must accept. By playing the game, you are showing that you accept them:

1. Your iForest character (which you access through a login name) is not your property, and has no value. It may be deleted or altered by the game administrator at any time.

2. You will not use a login name that is offensive or antisocial. If you do this your character may be deleted.

3. You will not play the game when common-sense says that you should not. For example, do not play when you are driving or doing anything that requires concentration.
