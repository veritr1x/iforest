The Mountains and the caves

The mountains lie north of Forestown, up a steep winding path. It quickly gets cold the higher you climb, and before long you are surrounded by deep snow.

The residents of the mountains are mostly non-human, and live underground. To find them, you will need to leave the path and work your way through narrow tunnels. 



The underground tunnel complex is huge, much larger than the whole of Forestown. The deeper you go, the nastier the inhabitants become, and the more interesting the weapons they are guarding.

Somewhere at the top of the one of the mountains there is the start of a ski-run and a game marker. There is also a text-camera up there, so everybody can see you've made it to the top.
