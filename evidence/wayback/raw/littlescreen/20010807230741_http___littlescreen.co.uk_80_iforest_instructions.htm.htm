Instructions

To play iForest you need a WAP mobile telephone.  To use the game properly you need to understand the rules and know a bit about the world where you will be playing.

If you want to skip this and learn about controlling the game, click here.

To visit Forestown's tourist information centre, click here.

The world of iForest is quite simple. There are a number of players, each of which can move around, pick up objects and use them. You can move as often as you like, and don't need to wait for other players to have their turn.

You can pick up any object that isn't too heavy and put it into your pockets. If your pockets are full, you will need to drop something before picking anything else up. Once you're holding an object you can use it, depending on what it can do.
