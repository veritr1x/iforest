Forestown was a peaceful settlement in a far away land. The inhabitants rarely ventured far outside its protective walls because of what was said to live there.

Recently however, rumors of dragons, fairies and magical sandwiches have drawn adventurers from around the world. Nowadays Forestown is crowded with tourists, who fight and steal from each other, and annoy the wolves in the forest.

Very few visitors stray far from the town, up into the mountains, and into the still uncharted regions beyond...
