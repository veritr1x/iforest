Instructions
You can't play the game from a web browser. Point your phone's WAP browser at wap.useeverything.com and choose "Vampire Country". If you don't know how to do this, consult your phone's manual.

Before you start, you will be asked for two names. The first should be your own. The second should be the name of the person you would like to rescue ("your friend" in the story). Enter the names by clicking on the text entry boxes (they probably look something like this [...]), and use the phone's keypad. When you're done, click OK.
