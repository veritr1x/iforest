What is it?

UseEverything.com is a text adventure game that runs on WAP-enabled mobile telephones. 

If you don't know what I'm talking about, click here.

 

The main features of the game are:

It's FREE! Well, UseEverything.com doesn't charge you anything. Your mobile phone operator might charge you for WAP calls.

No accounts or registration. To start playing, you just need to enter your first name, and off you go. When you get fed-up, just bookmark your position and come back later.

Two characters. The game's designed to be played in short stints. To stop you getting bored when you're stuck, you have two characters to control. When you can't figure out what one character is supposed to do next, switch to the other.
