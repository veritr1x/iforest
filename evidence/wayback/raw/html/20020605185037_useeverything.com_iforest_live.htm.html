Live Text Cameras

There are cameras scattered around the iForest world, which let you have a look at what's going on. As iForest is a text-based world, the cameras show you text instead of pictures.

Click here to open up a camera window. There are five cameras to choose between. You must have an iForest login to access this area.

Game Markers

See who currently has their flag in the various game markers. Any markers that haven't been found yet by anybody won't be listed. You must have an iForest login to access this area.
