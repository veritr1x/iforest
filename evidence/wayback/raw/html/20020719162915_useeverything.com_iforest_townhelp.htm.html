Forestown area

The town is where you start the game. There is a main street that leads north and south, with various public buildings on either side. If you head up the hill from the main square, you will come to the Forestown Mall, where you can buy refreshments and articles of clothing.

East from the main square is the lost property loop. This is a building where the possessions of those who have been hurt in battle are put. If at any time during the game you find yourself hospitalised, you should find that some of the objects you were holding before you were hurt have been moved here.
