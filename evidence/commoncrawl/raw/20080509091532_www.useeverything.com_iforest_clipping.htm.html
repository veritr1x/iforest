Character information for Palm OS® Handhelds.

If you have a PDA that runs Palm OS and can handle web-clipping applications, download the free iForest Character Information app.

This will give you live information about your iForest character, including where you are, what you are carrying, and what has happened to you since you stopped playing. If you need to pick up any mail from the post office, you can read this here too.

 

Download it now! - it's only 1.7K!

Each time you hit the "Info" or "Refresh" button, it may download several kilobytes of data. If you pay per-byte for web-clipping services (as many people do), please bear this in mind.
