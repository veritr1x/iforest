The Mansion

The mansion is where the greedy owner of Forestown lives. It lies at the northern end of the town, surrounded by a high wall and guarded 24 hours a day. Only those who can produce the Baron's seal can enter.

None of the locals have been inside the mansion for years, and have not seen the baron for quite a while. Some speculate that he's not in the house at all, but it is impossible to tell.

Inside the mansion, the most important thing to do is explore all the rooms, including the wine cellars underneath the kitchen. These are rumoured to be extensive and lead for several miles.
